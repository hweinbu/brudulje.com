About Me

Hi, I'm Helene, an artist seeking to recreate something precious to me, yet unnameable, through different media and techniques, both digital and analog.

At the core of everything I create, is the need to bring to life something that is somehow both tangible and intangible at the same time. My whole life I've dreamed of entering this alternate world, and in want of a way there, I have decided to make my own.

This website is my portfolio and blog, where you can get a glimpse into that world.
