About Me

I'm Helly von Creativity, a multi-disciplined artist who finds inspiration in the intersection of different creative mediums. My work spans across writing, graphic design, and photography, each discipline informing and enriching the others.

Whether I'm crafting narratives that blur the lines between reality and fiction, creating digital art that captures urban abstractions, or photographing the fleeting moments of light and shadow in everyday life, I'm driven by a curiosity to explore and express the world through multiple lenses.

My creative process is fluid and interconnected ‚Äì a photograph might inspire a story, a story might become a graphic piece, and a graphic design might lead me back to the streets with my camera. This cross-pollination of ideas is at the heart of everything I create.
